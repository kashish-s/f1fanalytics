Lapwise Leaderboard Simulation - Austria 2023

This tool is a laptime simulation visualization for Formula 1 races. It provides an interactive analysis of the performance of the top 3 racers for each lap. The positions of the top 3 drivers at the end of the selected lap are visually displayed on the track, and additional details such as lap times and driver information are presented in a tabular format below the visualization. The tool enables racing enthusiasts to explore and compare the racing dynamics of the leading drivers throughout the race.
